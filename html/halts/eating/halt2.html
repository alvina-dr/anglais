[Eating] Health through food

In this period of global pandemic and containment, boosting your immune system, improving your sleep, taking care of yourself ... are essential and increasingly common actions, where our physical and psychological form are put to test. But how can we take care of ourselves and our family easily? It's simple ! It is possible by paying attention to what we eat, to our food. Here are the foods that are good for your health, and to which special attention must be paid during this confinement !

The juice of a single lemon almost entirely covers our daily vitamin C requirement. In addition, lemon helps strengthen bones, reduces the appearance of certain cancer cells… Nuts and Seeds are rich in omega-3 acids, antioxidants and phytosterols, allowing the body to fight against the appearance of certain cancers, reduce cholesterol, as well as the risk of diabetes and heart disease. Garlic, antibacterial, antifungal, antiviral, antibiotic, garlic is one of the most complementary foods for health. But for these healing abilities to work well, you need to eat up to 6 cloves of garlic per week.

There are still other foods that are good for your health, it can be broccoli, spinach, avocados, apples ... All of them have a particularity, but all of them are good for your health. To conclude, food plays a decisive role in protecting our health. All scientific studies show that a varied and balanced diet helps to limit the risk of cancer, cardiovascular disease, hypercholesterolemia, osteoporosis, obesity and diabetes. And during confinement, we can’t move as our body needs, sunbathe, etc ... It’s necessary to eat well.
