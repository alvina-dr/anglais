[Universe] Mars Colonization

The main mission that makes astronauts vibing is the colonization of Mars.

Since the colonization of the moon, this mission is the main objective. Nasa plans the beginning of the mission for 2033 but it’s very optimistic. The problem is that Mars is far away from Earth, so the trip will last between 360 and 610 days.

There is a kind of race to conquer Mars, some countries are involved in “Human Mission to Mars” : The United States / China / European Union.

The mission is risky for the astronauts and very expensive. That’s why they must limit them as much as possible. The main risks of this mission are solar flares which can cause cancer, cosmics rays and other ionizing radiation whose we don’t know the effects because it is difficult to reproduce cosmic rays on earth without having an influence on health and weightlessness that include bone mineral density loss and eyesight impairment.
