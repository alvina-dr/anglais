[Universe] ISS (International Space Station)

The International Space Station is maybe the most important foundation for space research.

The ISS is a research laboratory orbiting around Earth. The laboratory studies Cancer research, fluid physics, Earth observation and even preparation for future space trips are part of the crew's activities. The main objective is learning things about space and how it works. The project was launched in 1983 by the United States president Ronald Reagan but the political context was not favorable for the space program and the project was very expensive.
