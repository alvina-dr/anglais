[Universe] Space Danger

A stop that aims to talk about the different dangers of space.

Radioactivity, solar wind, Near Earth Object (NEO) are on the program. NEO is an asteroid or comet in the solar system that its orbit around the Sun brings within a short distance of Earth's orbit, and therefore potentially close to Earth. Considering their mass and speed, NEOs can cause major human catastrophe, possibly global, even though the probability of such an impact is extremely low.
