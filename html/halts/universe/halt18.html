[Universe] Budget Défense > Spatial

Another important reason to justify the colonization of space is the continuous effort to increase the knowledge and technological capacities of humanity which could advantageously replace negative competitions like war. The Mars Society states for example:

“You have to go to Mars, because it's a formidable challenge. Civilizations need to measure up to such challenges in order to develop. War has long played this role. We must now find other reasons for overruns. "

Indeed, the space budget is much lower than that of defense. For example, taking the case of the United States in 2008, an estimate of $ 845 billion in direct costs was made from the Iraq war158. By comparison, the Hubble Space Telescope cost $ 2 billion and NASA's annual budget is $ 16 billion. The priority given to the war in Iraq is a misuse of the federal budget, according to USA Today columnist and founder Allen Neuharth159.
