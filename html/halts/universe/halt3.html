[Universe] The German Project

Here we are talking about the project v1 and v2 of Germany during the second world war.

This project aims to create missiles to "bring peace". This stop allows the road to see how we start from the war for peace with the conquest of space. So, these weapons are part of "Wunderwaffe", "miracle" weapons to change the war. However, their production costs too much for the Reich, and will not change its fate.

The chief engineer: Wernher von Braun will join NASA for civilian projects.
