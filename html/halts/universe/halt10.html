[Universe] Conspiracy Theory

Since the conquest of the moon, people are no longer interested in the conquest of space and its future.

The lack of information leads them to believe certain theories about the conquest of space. For some people, the conquest of the moon is a business effect aimed at selling more television, a new technology at the time that few people had. Moreover, others believe that the state lies to us and manipulates us about space. For them, the earth is flat and astronauts are actors employed by NASA to lie to us about STATE secrets. Satellites are also said to be a lie and the images are rigged to cover up this secrecy.
