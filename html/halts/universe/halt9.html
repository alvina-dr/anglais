[Universe] Moon Objective

Stop which sums up the Apollo mission, what it brings.

It also makes it possible to see the end of the space race and the cold war. It is a central stopover because it is a small step towards peace with the conquest of space.Apollo 11 is a mission of the American Apollo space program during which, for the first time, men landed on the Moon on July 21, 1969.

The American space agency, NASA, thus fulfills the objective set by President John F. Kennedy in 1961 to land a crew on the moon before the end of the 1960s. The aim was to demonstrate the superiority of the United States over the Soviet Union, which had been undermined by Soviet successes at the start of the space age in the context of the cold war which then opposed these two countries.
