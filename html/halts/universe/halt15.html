[Universe] Human race preservation

If a planetary catastrophe threatened the survival of the human species on Earth, a self-sufficient colony (a generational rocket) could "retro-colonize" Earth and restore civilization. Physicist Stephen Hawking also said :

â€œI don't think the human race will survive the next few thousand years unless we expand into space. There are too many accidents that can destroy life on one planet. But I am optimistic. We will reach for the stars. "

For him, allowing the human race to be confined to one planet puts them at the mercy of any disaster like an asteroid collision or nuclear war that could lead to mass extinction. Colonization of the Solar System would only be a first step before the search for another planet with conditions as favorable as Earth in another planetary system, around another star.

Likewise, the colonization of other solar systems would make it possible to escape the programmed destruction of our Sun, and the colonization of other galaxies would make it possible to survive in the event of a collision between galaxies.
