[Universe] N1 Rocket

Russian N-class rockets are the main launchers of the USSR. This stop allows to approach in detail the Russian program, the method of construction of their rockets, how it tested them.

Finally, we will see why the Russian program failed. The N1 rocket is the masterpiece of Russian engineering. This launcher was created to bring men to the moon. This program is directed by Korolev. It is this genius who directs the entire Russian space program. However this rocket has a weak point in its design. In fact, the Russians, to save time, carry out a single full-scale global test. During this test, the computer programs of the rocket will have a bug and will cut some of the reactors. The rocket will crash and cause the Russians to lose several years of research.

This failed test allowed the Americans to win the race for the moon.
