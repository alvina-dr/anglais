[Universe] Space Dead

This stop allows you to tackle the accidents and deaths of the conquest of space.

The lost cosmonauts or ghost cosmonauts is the name given to a conspiracy theory according to which cosmonauts died without being declared. These rumors use the secret reputation of the Soviet Union. These rumors are linked to the climate of secrecy surrounding the entire Soviet space program. Unlike NASA, it was customary for Soviet authorities to announce space missions only after successful completion. The failures were silent.
