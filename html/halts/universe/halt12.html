[Universe] Space X

Space X is an American company in the field of astronautics and space flight.

Founded on May 6, 2002 by Elon Musk, Space X is one of two private providers to which the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) has awarded a contract to transport cargo to the International Space Station (ISS). The company is also developing space exploration projects to the Moon and Mars, and the Starlink program for high-speed Internet access by satellites on Earth.
