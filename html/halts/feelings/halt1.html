[Feelings] Feelings in Japanese Culture

Japanese culture is very rich and unique from other cultures. Known for rigor, politeness, and respect. They are very calm and avoid expressing their emotions in public. Their very refined art of living combined with this expression from Zen Buddhism that the Japanese often like to recall "If speech is silver, silence is golden", give to these people all its charm and authenticity. Japanese people are rather discreet about their feelings but it is part of their culture.
