Regarding this issue, more and more eco-districts have been built from scratch across the world. The purpose of these districts are to reduce the CO2 emissions of each building composing them. To achieve that, many ecologic solutions are used, and more are developed with time. For example, the buildings are powered by solar panels so they don't produce radioactive detrimental emissions, they recycle rain water by filtering it and making it drinkable. These districts have a lot of green : trees everywhere, walls of vegetation, rivers. They also have everything a family needs to live happily : parks, shops and schools. There is for example a district like this in Bordeaux : The Ginko Eco-district.
