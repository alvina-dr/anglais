[Buildings] Technologic buildings

Buildings have always used the best of technology to push back the limits of what is possible. Humans have been searching for ways of building bigger and taller buildings through the ages. But now, technology has found itself another purpose in the construction business. In miami, people buying or renting a building such as a house or an apartment now have a choice regarding the payment of their bills. They can pay with traditional money such as the dollar, or they can pay using crypto-currencies, and more specifically, bitcoins.

The Bitcoin, a non-material currency worth a lot a money, is rising to be the best crypto-currency in the world, so real-estate workers have jumped on the occasion to make something new, and offer new ways of investing in stone.
