[Buildings] Historic buildings

There are across the world multiple constructions that almost every human knows. It can be for example Petra, Versailles, La Tour Eiffel, or the Great Wall of China. These are well known buildings that are famous by their names, the way they look, or even their sizes. They have such a public and cultural success that people will instantly picture them in their head when you talk about them

They are also famous because of their cultural background, and the history that they represent. Versailles is a symbol of the french royalty, while the Great Wall of China is a symbol of the power of chinese people in antiquity.
