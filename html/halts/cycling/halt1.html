[Cycling] Red Bull Rampage

The red bull rampage is a mountain bike freeride event that takes place in the United States, in the Utah Desertâ€™s canyons. It was created in 2001, but was judged too dangerous in 2004 after a lot of crashes and injuries. It was brought back in 2008 and has been happening every year since. During this event, the riders and their team have a whole week to design, dig and construct their line.

After this week, the riders have two runs to perform in front of a huge crowd and a jury. The jury rates the riders run by three main criteria : the line choice, the flow of the run, and the acrobatic tricks. It is one, if not the most dangerous bike event in the world.
